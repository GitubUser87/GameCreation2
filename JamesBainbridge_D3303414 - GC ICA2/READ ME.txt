CONTROLS:
Keyboard:
W + Up Arrow - Forward
A + Left Arrow - Left
D + Right Arrow - Right
S + Down Arrow - Down
Mouse - Camera Movement
E - Interact
Space Bar - Jump
P - Pause
Crtl - Crouch
R - Reload
Left Mouse Button - Shoot


Controller:
Left Control Stick - WASD - Movement
Right Control Stick - Camera Movement
A - Jump
X - Interact
Start Button - Pause
Left Trigger- Crouch
Right Shoulder Trigger - Reload
Right Trigger - Shoot

NOTES:
All buttons on widgets can only be used by mouse and cursor. (This relates to the menu widgets.)
In the tutorial all the hints will be in the top left Corner.
